About

Strike Zone is a family friendly batting park park located in Batters Ridge, VA. We offer a variety of packages that you and your family can choose from when you come to visit. Wheather you're hitting home runs in the batting cages, or spending time in the arcade, Strike Zone welcomes you!

All Platinum Packages include unlimited sessions to the batting cages. Each session will be limited to 1hr. Check with customer service to purchase packaged or be added to the batting list!
